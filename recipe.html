The Perfect PB & J

Description

A peanut Butter sandwich is the great sandwich to mankind! YOu can never go wrong with PB & J

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 jar of grape Jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy Peanut Butter
  • butter knife
  • sharp knife
  • cutting Board

Directions

  1. Collect your ingredients
  2. Spread peanut butter evenly onto one slice of bread using a knife
  3. Spread jelly or jam evenly onto the other slice of bread
  4. Press the two slices of bread together.
  5. Cut the sandwich
  6. joy your easy and yummy looking sandwich!
